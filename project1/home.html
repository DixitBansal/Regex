About Us

Kirloskar Technologies, popularly known as KTPL and a part of the 125 years old, multi-billion US dollars conglomerate â€“ Kirloskar Group, has been a successful healthcare enterprise for more than twenty five years. KTPL represented known global manufacturers of high-tech medical devices in India, offering a multi-disciplinary product portfolio catering to the fields of Neurosurgery, Nuclear Medicine, Oncology, Radiography, Vascular Surgery, Minimal Invasive Surgery and Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery. KTPL is fulfilling the needs of Indian health sector by equipping the hospitals with latest high-tech medical equipment.

KTPL is in the forefront in Medical Assets Management sector. It is actively engaged in planning & maintaining medical equipment of all kinds in small, major and tertiary hospitals across India.
